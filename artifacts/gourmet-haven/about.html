Who We Are

A Restaurant Built on Love for Food

It all started in 2009, when founder and head chef Karim Hossain returned to Dhaka after a decade training in Paris, Lyon, and Tokyo. He had a simple but ambitious dream: to create a space where world-class culinary craft meets the warmth and generosity of Bangladeshi hospitality.

Starting with just 12 seats and a hand-written menu, Gourmet Haven quickly earned a loyal following. Word spread — not through advertising, but through the language every great restaurant speaks: extraordinary food, made with genuine care.

Today, we seat 120 guests across three dining rooms and a private events space, but the spirit remains unchanged. Every dish tells a story. Every meal creates a memory.

Our Promise

What Makes Us Different

We believe great dining is about more than food — it's about how you feel when you leave.

Global Techniques, Local Heart

Our chefs trained across three continents but cook with the soul of Bangladesh. Every dish is a dialogue between the world's best culinary traditions and our local heritage.

Farm-to-Table Sourcing

We partner directly with local organic farms for produce harvested the same morning it reaches your plate. Every ingredient is chosen with intention — no compromises.

Genuine Hospitality

Our front-of-house team trains for 6 months before ever serving a guest. Service isn't a job title here — it's a calling. You'll feel the difference the moment you walk in.

Award-Winning Quality

Twelve industry awards and counting — but the only award that matters to us is the look on your face when the food arrives. We never stop raising our own bar.

Our Philosophy

Food is More Than Fuel — It's Connection

We believe the best restaurants are places where people feel completely at ease. Where the food is extraordinary but the atmosphere is warm and unpretentious. Where you lose track of time because the conversation is so good — and the food keeps giving you reasons to linger.

That's the experience we build every day at Gourmet Haven. Not a transaction, but a genuine moment of joy.
